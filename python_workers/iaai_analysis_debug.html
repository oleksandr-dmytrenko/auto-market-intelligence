    Buyer App Updates

    The latest updates to the IAA Buyer App were built to help you enhance your buyer experience with IAA.

    Learn More

    Cars

    Whether rebuilding or salvaging, exploring our expansive auto inventory is easy. In addition to basic criteria such as year, vehicle type (classics, SUV) or make and model, you can customize searches with details like damage type, title type (clear, salvage), transmission, or mileage. Using our Advanced Search can provide even more custom options so you can stop browsing and start bidding.

    Motorcycles

    Looking for parts for a rebuild to get back out there and enjoy the open road? Our large inventory of motorcycles is broken down into multiple distinct categories, including competition, cruiser, racer, roadster and touring. Set your preferred price and find exactly what you need at the most convenient location. Youll also find ATVs within our motorcycle listings.

    Pickup Trucks

    If youre searching for something specific in the pickup truck category, weve got it covered with everything from two-door chassis to four-door commercial to extended cabs. As with all our vehicle categories, you can filter your searches based on the type of loss claim or primary damage type before finding the right date, time and location for the vehicles next auction.

    Heavy Trucks

    If youre in need of a substantial size purchase in our heavy truck category, our inventory at multiple locations in Canada includes a variety of makes from the likes of Freightliner, Peterbilt, Kenworth, Mack and International. Filter your searches by manual or automatic transmission to find what suits your needs, and IAA can even assist with setting up transportation.

    Buses

    When it comes to buses, weve got a wide selection of everything from former school buses to light transports to large touring vehicles. Select your preferred manufacturer and filter your searches based on engine size and school vs. non-school to find what you need. Well even give you a choice when it comes to color. Looking for a red school bus? We might just have one.

    RV's

    Whether your needs are geared for warm or cold weather, searching through our RV inventory offers a wide variety of categories such as motor homes, boats, personal watercraft (jet skis, wave runners), snowmobiles and travel trailers. Choose from multiple manufacturers in each category and see whats available for bidding in your region.

    Trailers

    The trailers in our inventory come in multiple sizes and types for you to browse, including container chassis, dolly, dump, flatbed, and grain from dozens of manufacturers. Use our high-definition photos to get a complete look at all sides or interiors before choosing which trailer youd like to bid on. Picking up and hauling larger equipment out of our yards is safe and easy.
